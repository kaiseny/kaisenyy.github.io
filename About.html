About Us

Welcome to the Brave Little Abacus Fan Site, a website dedicated to documenting and celebrating the music of The Brave Little Abacus.

This site was created as a fan project to collect information about the band’s history, discography, and lyrics in one place. The site also includes a brief history of the band and other materials intended to help new listeners discover their music.
